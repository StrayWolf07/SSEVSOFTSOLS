About Us

Learn more about SSEV Softsols and our mission to innovate through technology.

M/s. SSEV SOFTWARE SOLUTIONS PRIVATE LIMITED is a subsidiary of SSEV Group with the concept of exploring the "Nature's Power” to provide cutting-edge technologies and AI interpretation. The primary objective is to create a niche in the Technology, Software, and Artificial Intelligence Sectors. The company has done research on one of the most burning and painful issues of “Traffic problems” across the world and developed some of the BEST solutions for Eco and safe traffic flows. Now the company has developed AI-based, most intelligent Integrated Traffic Management solutions. The Company is working on AI-based Integrated Asset Management solutions for providing real-time and enhanced plant life solutions for projects, plants, and infrastructure facilities. The company is making its contribution to improving farmers' revenue and growth by providing AI-based technology solutions in the farming sector. The company is managed by personnel with vast experience and young treks.
